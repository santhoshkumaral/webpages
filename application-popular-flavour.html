POPULAR FLAVOURS

There are reasons why popular flavours are popular;
we are adding more!

Dairy

We are keenly aware of the unique consistencies
that are essential for manifold dairy products
loved across the globe. We ensure to keep that
block of cheese and that melt in the mouth ice-
cream, taste like dream.

Brown

Constantly evolving with the changing trends of
consumers’ taste, we love innovating complex,
intense, bold and layered flavour components
for chocolate, coffee, nuts, cookies, biscuits,
toffee, fudge, cocoa and other delicious brown
flavours.

Fruit

The possibilities of crafting delicate, different fruit
flavours are virtually endless and that exhilarates
us to create fruit flavours of freshly-picked natural-
ingredient genuineness for various types of
industry uses.

Savoury

Authenticity comes second to none for the
ever-growing savoury industry. We develop
healthy and heady aromas, toppings, seasonings,
dressings, sprinklers etc for ready meals, meat,
soups, snacks, sauces and more.

Ethnic

The world is smaller than ever, and for audiences
of all geographies and ethnicities, we delightedly
create traditional flavours to alleviate their
nostalgias of their native foods - anything from
savouries to sweets, snacks to bakery.
