CreamR

Our manufacturing facility is coupled with in-house expertise of
flavourists that’s informed of our clients’ varying and complex needs. There is an increasing number
of consumers moving from dairy products to purer and more natural plant-based beverages for
protein and other essential nutrients. We adhere to and create the richness and
texture of the original products in the substitutes as well.

Properly formulated non-dairy creamer (NDC) will impart
desirable creamy or milky flavour, body, and colour to food. It
caters to that large segment of consumers who are lactose
intolerant. Our research penetrates to the molecular level of
ingredients we use so that we can cater to the unattended
section of non-dairy products consumers, too.

Our NDC range is a versatile, convenient, consistent and economical way of adding oil system into
food. It improves flavour, mouthfeel & appearance, and incorporates desired oil types & levels.
Excellent solubility and fat content between 30% and 50% are its other main benefits.
